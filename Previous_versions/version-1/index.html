🧠 Smart Walking Detection
Bloom uses a cadence-based algorithm — it only counts steps when your walking rhythm matches 1.4–3.5 Hz (natural human walking pace). Car vibrations, bus rides, and random movements are automatically filtered out.

Keep your phone in your pocket or hand while walking. The app keeps counting even in the background.
